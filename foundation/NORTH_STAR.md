# North Star

Status: Draft
Owner: Konstantin / Izumi IT
Purpose: Define the 5-10 year target architecture for Izumi IT.

## Target

Izumi IT becomes an AI-native product company serving the Japanese B2B market.

The company builds and operates multiple SaaS products with a clear company operating system, practical engineering standards, and knowledge that survives specific tools, AI providers, and individual sessions.

## Target architecture

In 5-10 years, Izumi IT should have:
- multiple SaaS products for Japanese B2B customers;
- LINE Business OS as the first major product platform;
- a clear operating system for strategy, product, engineering, sales, support, and operations;
- vendor independence from any single AI model, development tool, hosting provider, or SaaS vendor;
- scalable team processes that work beyond one founder;
- durable company knowledge stored in GitHub and other deliberate systems;
- workflows that can be taught, reviewed, improved, and reused;
- decision records that explain why important choices were made.

## AI-native operating principle

AI should increase execution quality and speed, but Izumi IT must remain responsible for decisions, reviews, customer promises, security, and final product quality.

## Long-term rule

Build toward the ideal company operating system progressively. Do not create abstract structure before it reduces repeated work, risk, or decision fatigue.
