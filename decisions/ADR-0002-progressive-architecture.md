# ADR-0002: Progressive Architecture

Status: Accepted
Owner: Konstantin / Izumi IT
Purpose: Record how IAOS will pursue long-term architecture without over-building too early.

## Context

Izumi IT has a long-term goal of becoming an AI-native product company with multiple SaaS products, scalable processes, vendor independence, and durable company knowledge.

The current reality is founder-led execution with time and budget constraints. IAOS must help current product work, especially LINE Business OS, without becoming bureaucracy.

## Decision

IAOS will pursue an ideal long-term architecture through practical incremental implementation.

Only create workflows, standards, prompts, and decisions when they reduce repeated work, risk, or decision fatigue.

## Consequences

IAOS will start small and become more complete through real product work.

Future structure should be compatible with the North Star, but current documents must remain useful now.

Temporary throwaway processes should be avoided when a simple durable workflow can be created instead.

Unused or outdated documents should be simplified, replaced, or archived.
